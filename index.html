Manchester United Football Clubcommonly referred to as Man United (often stylised as Man Utd), or simply United, is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, GreaterManchester, England. The club competes in the Premier League, the top division in the English football leaguesystem. Nicknamed the Red Devils, it was founded as Club in 1878, but changed its name to Manchester United in 1902. The club moved from NewtonHeath to its current stadium, Old Trafford, in 1910.
